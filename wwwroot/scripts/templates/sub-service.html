Audio and Video Collaboration

Go Back

OIT provides a suite of audio/video collaboration and conferencing tools enabling employees to collaborate and share information real time with team members or external stakeholders, whether participating in standard audio conference, or meeting face to face over a video connection.

Solutions provided are:

  • WebEx - a web conferencing solution with audio, video, and real-time content sharing.
  • MS Teams - an enterprise application offering powerful collaboration tools such as chat, audio & video conference, and desktop sharing.

  • Polycom or GCI Audio Bridge
  • WebEx in Anchorage, Fairbanks, Juneau
  • WebEx in all other Alaska locations
  • MS Teams

  • Core Services Bundle

  • Employee Dashboard - WebEx Resources

  • For support please request help at AlaskaNow OIT Portal.
